# Preferences

Classes for customizing the reader experience.

## EPUB Preferences

[EPUBPreferences] controls visual appearance:
- Typography (font family, size, line height)
- Colors (background, text)
- Layout (margins, column count)
- Reading direction

## TTS Preferences

[TTSPreferences] controls text-to-speech:
- Speech rate
- Pitch
- Voice selection

## Audio Preferences

[AudioPreferences] controls audiobook playback:
- Playback rate
- Skip intervals
